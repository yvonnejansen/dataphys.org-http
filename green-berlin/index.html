2017 – Green Berlin

 

Tangible data visualization of green areas and water in Berlin.

"Green Berlin" is a living map showing parks and forests in Berlin. The green areas on the wooden map are laser cut, with moss growing through the holes.

Source: Sebastian Meier, Green Berlin

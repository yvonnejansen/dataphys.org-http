2008 – Andreas Fischers' Data Sculptures

     

Andreas Nicolas Fischer is a Berlin-based artist. Above are four of his 3-D data sculptures:

  1. Fundament (world GDP and derivatives volume)
  2. Indizes (finance data over time)
  3. A week in the life (cellphone communications)
  4. Reflection (FFT of a music piece).

Source: http://anfischer.com via infosthetics.

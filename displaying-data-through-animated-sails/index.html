2009 – DataMorphose: Animated Sails

  

DataMorphose is an interactive installation which projects various data streams into real space and visualizes it three-dimensionally. Information is represented by spanned and moving sails directly in the room.

Source: Christiane Keller (2009). Data Morphose.

What are appropriate submissions? Although we try to be as inclusive as possible, by physical visualization we mean a data-driven physical artefact. So it has to be both physical (i.e., not exclusively rely on flat media like paper+ink or computer displays) and data-driven (like common data visualizations – see discussion here). We also include related artefacts that can help understand physical visualizations or can help create novel ones. We are especially seeking old/historical artefacts, as well as pragmatic artefacts, since these are much less common and relatively hard to find.

Please follow our instructions below for optimal formatting. Your submission will be processed more rapidly if you do. Thank you very much for your contribution!

2014 – #Good vs. #Evil

  

A race is going on between two Twitter hashtags, materialized by two cars. The blue car represents #Good, the red car represents #Evil.

Source: Patrick Keller (2014). I&IC Workshop #3 at ECAL: output > “Botcaves” / Networked Data Objects.

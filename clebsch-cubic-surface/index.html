2006 – Clebsch Cubic Surface

  

A 90 cm tall Clebsch bicubic surface hand made in resin and fiber. It includes all lines lying inside the surface.

Also see our entry 1880 – Klein’s Mathematical Plaster Models for an earlier version made of plaster.

2015 – Canadian Federal Election Explained with LEGOs

  

On Canadian national news, Toronto-based artist and organiser Dave Meslin used LEGO bricks to illustrate the results of the 2015 federal election. He shows election results based on a first-past-the-post system, and compares them to a representation illustrating what results would look like if based on proportional representation.

Source:  CBC News (2015) If Canada had proportional representation: Dave Meslin shows with Lego.
